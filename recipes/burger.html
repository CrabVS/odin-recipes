Burger

Description

This is a very tasty burger. The perfect burger.

A flat round mass of minced meat or vegetables, which is fried and often eaten in a bread roll.

Ingredients

  • Patty of Ground Beef
  • Two Buns
  • Tomato
  • Lettuce
  • Onion
  • Cheese
  • Your Favorite Sauce

Steps

  1. Cut the bun into two halves
  2. Heat up the pan with a bit of oil inside.
  3. Cook the patty in the pan.
  4. When the patty is nearly done, add a strip of cheese on top of it to get it to melt a bit
  5. Add the patty onto the bun
  6. Cook the tomato and onion stripes very briefly and add them on top of the patty
  7. Wash and add the lettuce
  8. Add the sauce
